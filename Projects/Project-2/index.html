How to Make a Chocolate Mousse Cake

  • Ingredients
  • Cake
  • Mousse
  • Ganache
  • Assembly

Ingredients:

  • Cake:
  • 5 cups cake flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened dutch-processed cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 pound unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Mousse:
  • 4 ounces semisweet chocolate
  • 4 large eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Ganache:
  • 24 ounces semisweet chocolate
  • 2 cups heavy cream

Equipment:

  • Stand Mixer
  • 2x 9in. Cake pans
  • Parchment paper
  • Serrated knife
    • Small pot
    • A bowl that fits over the pot
