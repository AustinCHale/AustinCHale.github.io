About Us

We are not your typical taxidermy that you would find on the walls of a hunting store, but artistic and unique renditions of animals. Each taxidermy piece is crafted with attention to detail. From intricately posed small mammals to elegantly preserved birds, the work is not only technically proficient but also artistically innovative. We often incorporate natural elements like dried flowers, reclaimed wood, and antique frames to create striking displays that make each piece unique to itself.
